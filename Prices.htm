Walk-On Package

For the individual player up to ten players. Come by yourself or bring your friends.

Group Package

Perfect for a bunch of friends meeting up and having some fun. Requires 10 or more players.

Church/Corp. Package

Great for the preplanned church outing or corporate teambuilding. Requires 14 or more players and payment under one invoice,

Birthday Parties

Designed for hosting and paying for the whole birthday. A minimum of 9 players (birthday person + 8 or more) is required. We even throw in a free package and a paintball grenade to the birthday player!

Weekday Groups

Requiring 14 or more players this package is a favorite for company employees wanting to get out of their element and have a good time. It's a wonderful team building sport. All play will be private.

Pee Wee Parties

We offer an exciting way for 7-10 year olds to play using a smaller paintball set at a slower velocity. Designed for the younger player, parents and older siblings can join in too. Required 10 or more players.

About our Packages

We use the highest quality paintballs available. All gear is properly washed and sanitized before use. A detailed safety orientation will be given to all players. Our insured field exceeds safety requirements...

It can be a bit confusing the first time booking at Paintball Command. If you have from 1 to 9 players coming to play, your only option is one of the two Walk-On packages. For ten or more, with everyone paying for themselves, select the Group Pricing. The Birthday and Church/Corporate Group pricing is for organized groups that have collected money beforehand and will pay under one form of payment. For all players who are under 10 years old, the Pee Wee Party is your only option. And finally, if you book a weekday then choose the Weekday Group Package. Except for Walk-Ons, all packages require a deposit to secure your spot. Call or e-mail with questions!

Explore through the package(s) that suit you and when your ready to book, select the "Book Now" splat. It will ask for a date then bring you to the package selection. Choose the appropriate one and follow the prompts. After booking, you receive a confirmation e-mail. Make sure all players have a waiver before your play day.

You can bring any food or drink you want except alcoholic beverages. We keep 5 gallon coolers of water in our staging, have a snack and drink machine, and serve lunches for a nominal price. Pizza is also available for delivery from a few places.
